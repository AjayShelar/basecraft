Privacy Policy for Google Ads (BaseCraft)

Effective Date: 1st January 2025

BaseCraft ("we," "our," "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our website and our Google Advertising (Google Ads) campaigns.

By using our website, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect Through Google Ads

When you visit https://basecraft.in/, we may collect certain information automatically through Google advertising tools, including:

  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • IP address (approximate location)
  • Website activity (pages visited, time spent)
  • Referral source (how you reached our site)
  • Interactions with ads (clicks, conversions)

This information is collected using cookies, pixels, and similar tracking technologies.

2. How We Use Google Ads Data

We use Google Ads and related tracking features to:

  • Measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns
  • Understand how users interact with our website after clicking ads
  • Improve targeting and marketing performance
  • Serve relevant ads to potential customers interested in MVP development, consulting, and software services

3. Google Ads Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Google and its partners may use cookies to:

  • Show BaseCraft ads across Google Search, YouTube, and partner websites
  • Provide ad personalization based on browsing behavior
  • Track conversions (for example, when you submit a contact form)

Google may combine this data with information from other websites you visit.

Learn more about how Google uses data: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Remarketing and Personalized Advertising

BaseCraft may use Google Remarketing to advertise to users who have previously visited our website.

This means:

  • You may see our ads again on Google or YouTube
  • Ads are served based on anonymous cookie identifiers
  • We do not personally identify you through remarketing

You can opt out of personalized advertising here: https://adssettings.google.com/

5. Conversion Tracking

We use Google Ads Conversion Tracking to understand when users take actions such as:

  • Submitting an inquiry form
  • Clicking a call-to-action button
  • Contacting BaseCraft after viewing an ad

This helps us measure campaign performance. Conversion tracking does not provide us with personally identifying information unless you submit it directly.

6. Data Sharing with Google

Google acts as a third-party service provider for advertising and analytics.

Information collected via Google Ads cookies may be processed by Google under their privacy policies.

Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

BaseCraft does not sell your personal data to third parties.

7. Your Choices and Controls

You can manage or disable cookies through:

  • Your browser settings
  • Google Ad Settings (opt-out of personalization): https://adssettings.google.com/
  • Google Analytics opt-out tool: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout/
