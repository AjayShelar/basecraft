Our Services

At BaseCraft, we offer a range of services to help startups turn their ideas into reality, ensuring they have the right foundations for scalability and success.

1. MVP Software Development

We specialize in building Minimum Viable Products (MVPs) to help startups validate their ideas and get to market faster. Our MVP development services are tailored to meet the unique needs of your product and business goals.

  • a) Web: Development of responsive, fast, and scalable web applications that focus on core features to help you validate your business idea with real users.
  • b) Android and iOS App: Creation of mobile applications for both Android and iOS platforms, designed for seamless performance and user engagement from day one.

2. Software Architecture

We provide software architecture services to ensure that your system is built to scale, perform efficiently, and evolve with your needs as your business grows.

  • a) Foundational Architecture: We design the core architecture of your application, laying the foundation for performance, scalability, and long-term maintenance.

3. Tech Lead Advisory

As part of our advisory services, we offer expert guidance on architecture, code quality, and solution design to ensure that your tech decisions are aligned with your goals.

  • a) Architecture Review: We assess and provide feedback on your existing system architecture to ensure it's scalable, maintainable, and well-suited to meet your product's long-term needs.
  • b) Code Review: We conduct in-depth code reviews to ensure that best practices are followed, and that your codebase is clean, efficient, and scalable.
  • c) Solution Designs: We help you design technical solutions that meet your business requirements while ensuring that the design is robust, scalable, and easy to maintain.
